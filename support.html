Safety Light — Support

A bright flashlight and high-visibility colour signal light for iPhone and Apple Watch.

Quick help

  • Pick a colour, pick a flash mode, tap Turn on. The controls hide so the light fills the screen — tap the screen to bring them back, or tap Turn off.
  • Use the Screen / Flashlight / Both control to choose the light source — the colour screen, the rear LED torch, or both at once.
  • The screen stays awake while the light is on, so it won’t auto-lock. Locking your phone turns the light off (a normal iPhone limitation).
  • The Apple Watch app works on its own and uses the watch screen as the light (the watch has no LED flashlight).
  • Strobe and Blink flash rapidly — avoid them if you or anyone nearby is sensitive to flashing light.
