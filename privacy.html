Privacy Policy — Safety Light

Last updated: 14 July 2026

Safety Light does not collect any data.

  • We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.
  • The app has no account and no login.
  • The app makes no network connections and works fully offline.
  • The app requests no permissions — no camera, microphone, location, contacts, or photos.
  • Your chosen colour and flash mode are saved only on your device (in local app settings) and never leave it.
  • We use no analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

Because we collect nothing, there is nothing to request, correct, or delete.

Payments

Payments are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store; we never see your payment information. See Apple’s Privacy Policy for how Apple processes purchases.
